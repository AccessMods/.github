# Welcome to the Accessibility Modding Hub

## üéÆ Our Mission
We are a community-driven initiative dedicated to collecting, organizing, and maintaining accessibility mods for mainstream video games. Our goal is to bridge the gap between visually impaired gamers and the titles they want to play by consolidating development efforts under one roof.

## üöÄ How It Works
We believe in open collaboration and stability. Our unique workflow allows developers to maintain ownership of their work while giving players a centralized, trusted source for mods.

1.  **Development:** Mods start in individual developers' repositories.
2.  **Stabilization:** Once a mod is tested and confirmed stable by the community, it moves here.
3.  **Consolidation:** The repository is transferred to this Organization, ensuring long-term maintenance and a central download location for players.

**For Players:** 
Check the **Repositories** tab to find supported games. Look for the "Releases" section on the right side of any repository to download the latest mod installer.
